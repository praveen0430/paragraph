Famous Cricketer

Rohit Sharma(1)

Sharma was born on April 30, 1987, in Bansod, Nagpur, in the state of Maharashtra, India.
He first joined a cricket camp in 1999, when coach Dinesh Lad, a friend of his uncle's, noticed his talent
Lad used to scout for talent in those days and was very impressed with the way Sharma bowled.

Virat Kohli(2)

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer
who is known for his batting, leadership, and discipline .
He is considered one of the greatest batsmen of all time.
